What are some situations when branches would be helpful in keeping your history
organized? How would branches help?

Branches let you take things in different paths, you can branch out from your
original ideas and try new things out without having to worry about breaking
your code. It lets you stay organized throughout your coding by giving your
version control some structure and organization.
